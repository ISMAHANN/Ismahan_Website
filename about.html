About

Completed dual undergraduate degrees in Geography and Child Development, with Geography as the primary field. Graduated top of the Geography program at İzmir Kâtip Çelebi University. Research interests include physical geography, GIS, natural hazards, earthquakes, and machine learning applications in geosciences. During undergraduate and graduate studies, participated in research projects, presented at national and international conferences, and published scientific work. Completed internships at Boğaziçi University Kandilli Observatory and TÜBİTAK.

Completed an MSc in Geography at Balıkesir University with a thesis on earthquake magnitude estimation using decision tree methods. Currently pursuing a PhD in Geography at the same institution, with additional training in programming, spatial analysis, GIS, and data science applications.
