About

However, as I currently have a thesis on geomorphology, my main focus is on my official diploma in geography. I graduated from both programs in July 2023. I will graduate as the top student of my geography program. I achieved a GPA of over 3.50, the highest grade in the program. My GPA is above 3.10 in my child development undergraduate program. In addition to all these, I presented three papers and three of my articles were published as abstracts, I attended many conferences and seminars, I learned and developed sign language. I was unofficially involved in a project called UNESCO World Heritage Sites. I did my internship at Boğaziçi University Kandilli Observatory. Finally, I wrote a project for the Izmir Universities Platform. And now, I'm studying physical geography master in Balikesir University.
