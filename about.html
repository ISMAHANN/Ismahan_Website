About

I graduated from two undergraduate degrees in July 2023, one in child development through distance education and one in geography face-to-face. My main focus is on my official diploma in geography. I graduated as the top student of my geography program with a GPA of over 3.50, the highest in the program. My GPA is above 3.10 in my child development undergraduate program.

In addition to my academic achievements, I presented three papers, published three abstracts, and attended numerous conferences and seminars. I also learned and developed sign language, participated unofficially in a project called UNESCO World Heritage Sites, and interned at Boğaziçi University Kandilli Observatory and TÜBİTAK. Moreover, I wrote a project for the Izmir Universities Platform. Now, I am pursuing a master's degree in physical geography at Balikesir University and am currently in the thesis stage. My master's thesis focuses on decision trees and earthquakes. Furthermore, I took computer and software courses through Pamukkale University.
